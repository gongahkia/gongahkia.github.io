Gabriel Ong is a second-year undergraduate student reading Computing and Law at Singapore Management University. Gabriel works as the tech development director and co-founder of The Yap Labs, a start-up based out of Singapore that builds products to gamify social interactions. Here, Gabriel works primarily on coding the cross-platform application Yap.com, the chief product of the organisation. In his free time, Gabriel enjoys writing software that benefits programmers, practising lawyers and law students. His latest efforts are in development of a DSL that provides a grammatically-valid representation of Singapore Criminal Law. Gabriel is interested in pursuing the entrepreneurship of legal technology in Singapore later in his career.
