Characteristic of Kirino's writing, *Real World* left me feeling cold all over *(and not to its detriment either)*.

Kirino is unparalleled when it comes to handling multiperspectivity to bundle together a terse, tightly plotted piece.

*Real World* left me with many of the same feelings of urban alienation and social animosity that her earlier *Out* did. If anything, the 6 years between these 2 works have honed her craft.

Do check this book out.
