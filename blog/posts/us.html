Us

Director
Jordan Peele
Release Year
2019
Rating
3.5/5
Date
27 Apr 2021

Another intriguing take on the horror film, Peele delivers a story that hits startlingly close to home for anyone suffering from imposter syndrome and those struggling to determine their identity. While I can't say I was able to understand and appreciate the full meaning of "Us", I enjoyed it enough as a piece of subversive horror.
