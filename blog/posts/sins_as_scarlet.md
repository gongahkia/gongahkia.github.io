Always riveting, *Sins as Scarlet* rivals and in many ways surpasses *Blue Light Yokohama* as a [police procedural](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Police_procedural), breathing gusts of raspy life into the weathered Inspector Iwata.

Obregón has never disappointed when it comes to the mystery genre, but the detailed pen he wields to flesh out the decrepid backgrounds his actors work within might be his crowning achievement.
