Shorter review today. All in all, I really enjoyed this book. While I’m already a fan of Murakami’s dual-narrative style, the way both narratives began to converge in the second half of Hard-Boiled Wonderland and the End of the World is really something else. The start is definitely a slog to get through though.

Godspeed.
