Awesome feeling to be back reading some more Higashino. Kaga as the protagonist is always entertaining, but *Newcomer* knocks it out of the park by focalising its core conceit from multiple perspectives. If anything, this book has convinced me that as good as Higashino is at constructing a thrilling narrative, he excels even more at character writing.
