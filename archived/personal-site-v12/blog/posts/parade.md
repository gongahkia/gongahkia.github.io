For the uninitiated, Tankas follow a 5-7-5-7-7 syllable scheme and are the older brother of the Haiku.

The review below is formatted as a Tanka.

*Few books have left me*

*As terrified of humans*

*Caught in a monsoon*

*Treading water at great cost*

*Forever unknowable*

Godspeed.
