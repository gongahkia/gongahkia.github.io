Sea Kayak arose from a very real need of mine, where I'd started to wish for a quick and portable way to microdose bite-sized Singaporean legal updates on a daily basis.

Over the process of refining Sea Kayak, I realised I wasn't the only person who felt this way. Building for others also incentivised me to prettify Sea Kayak's frontend from its more utilitarian first-pass.

It's now gotten to a place that I'm happy and proud of. Find it at https://sea-kayak.vercel.app/ if it sounds like something you'd be keen to check out.
